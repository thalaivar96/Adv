## CHAPTER 1: KINEMATICS

**Instantaneous Velocity**
$$\vec{v} = \frac{d\vec{r}}{dt}$$

**Instantaneous Acceleration**
$$\vec{a} = \frac{d\vec{v}}{dt} = \frac{d^2\vec{r}}{dt^2}$$

**Acceleration as a Function of Position**
$$a = v \frac{dv}{dx}$$

**Average Velocity**
$$\vec{v}_{avg} = \frac{\Delta \vec{r}}{\Delta t}$$

**Average Speed**
$$v_{speed, avg} = \frac{\text{Total Distance}}{\Delta t}$$

**First Equation of Motion (Constant Acceleration)**
$$\vec{v} = \vec{u} + \vec{a}t$$

**Second Equation of Motion (Constant Acceleration)**
$$\vec{s} = \vec{u}t + \frac{1}{2}\vec{a}t^2$$

**Third Equation of Motion (Constant Acceleration)**
$$v^2 = u^2 + 2\vec{a} \cdot \vec{s}$$

**Displacement in the $n^{\text{th}}$ Second**
$$s_n = u + \frac{a}{2}(2n-1)$$

**Time of Flight (Projectile Motion)**
$$T = \frac{2u \sin\theta}{g}$$

**Maximum Height (Projectile Motion)**
$$H = \frac{u^2 \sin^2\theta}{2g}$$

**Horizontal Range (Projectile Motion)**
$$R = \frac{u^2 \sin 2\theta}{g}$$

**Equation of Trajectory**
$$y = x \tan\theta - \frac{gx^2}{2u^2 \cos^2\theta}$$

**Equation of Trajectory (Range Form)**
$$y = x \tan\theta \left( 1 - \frac{x}{R} \right)$$

**Time of Flight on an Inclined Plane**
$$T_{incline} = \frac{2u \sin\alpha}{g \cos\beta}$$

**Range Up an Inclined Plane**
$$R_{up} = \frac{u^2[\sin(2\alpha+\beta) - \sin\beta]}{g \cos^2\beta}$$

**Radius of Curvature (Kinematic Vector Form)**
$$R_c = \frac{v^2}{a_\perp} = \frac{|\vec{v}|^3}{|\vec{v} \times \vec{a}|}$$

**Radius of Curvature (Calculus Form)**
$$R_c = \frac{[1 + (dy/dx)^2]^{3/2}}{|d^2y/dx^2|}$$

**Relative Velocity**
$$\vec{v}_{AB} = \vec{v}_A - \vec{v}_B$$

**Shortest Time to Cross a River**
$$t_{shortest} = \frac{d}{v_{br}}$$

**Drift in River Crossing**
$$x_{drift} = (v_r + v_{br} \sin\theta) \frac{d}{v_{br} \cos\theta}$$

**Velocity of Approach/Separation**
$$v_{app} = - \frac{dr}{dt} = \vec{v}_{rel} \cdot \hat{r}_{rel}$$

**Distance of Closest Approach**
$$d_{min} = \frac{|\vec{r}_{rel, 0} \times \vec{v}_{rel}|}{|\vec{v}_{rel}|}$$

## CHAPTER 2: NEWTON'S LAWS & FRICTION

**Newton's Second Law (Variable Mass System)**
$$\vec{F} = \frac{d\vec{p}}{dt} = m\vec{a} + \vec{v}\frac{dm}{dt}$$

**Impulse-Momentum Theorem**
$$\vec{J} = \int \vec{F} dt = \Delta \vec{p}$$

**Thrust Force on a Rocket**
$$\vec{F}_{thrust} = \vec{v}_{rel} \left( -\frac{dm}{dt} \right)$$

**Rocket Equation (Velocity at any instant)**
$$v = u + v_{rel} \ln \left( \frac{m_0}{m} \right) - gt$$

**Virtual Work / String Constraint Equation**
$$\sum \vec{T} \cdot \vec{v} = 0 \implies \sum \vec{T} \cdot \vec{a} = 0$$

**Wedge / Normal Constraint**
$$\vec{a}_A \cdot \hat{n} = \vec{a}_B \cdot \hat{n}$$

**Hooke's Law (Spring Force)**
$$F_s = -kx$$

**Equivalent Spring Constant (Series Combination)**
$$k_{series} = \frac{k_1 k_2}{k_1 + k_2}$$

**Equivalent Spring Constant (Parallel Combination)**
$$k_{parallel} = k_1 + k_2$$

**Pseudo Force (Non-Inertial Frame)**
$$\vec{F}_{pseudo} = -m\vec{a}_{frame}$$

**Static Friction Limit**
$$f_s \leq \mu_s N$$

**Kinetic Friction**
$$f_k = \mu_k N$$

**Angle of Friction / Angle of Repose**
$$\tan \lambda = \mu_s$$

**Minimum Force Required to Pull a Block**
$$F_{\text{min pull}} = \frac{\mu mg}{\sqrt{1+\mu^2}}$$

**Centripetal Acceleration**
$$a_c = \frac{v^2}{R} = \omega^2 R$$

**Tangential Acceleration**
$$a_t = \frac{dv}{dt} = R\alpha$$

**Optimum Banking Speed (No Friction Required)**
$$v_{opt} = \sqrt{Rg \tan\theta}$$

**Maximum Safe Speed on a Banked Road**
$$v_{max} = \sqrt{Rg \left( \frac{\mu + \tan\theta}{1 - \mu \tan\theta} \right)}$$

**Minimum Velocity at Lowest Point to Complete Vertical Circle**
$$v_{\text{min loop}} = \sqrt{5gR}$$

**Tension Equation in Vertical Circular Motion**
$$T - mg \cos\theta = \frac{mv^2}{R}$$

## CHAPTER 3: WORK, ENERGY & POWER

**Work Done by a Variable Force**
$$W = \int \vec{F} \cdot d\vec{r}$$

**Work-Energy Theorem**
$$W = \Delta K$$

**Conservation of Mechanical Energy**
$$W_{nc} + W_{ext} = \Delta E_{mech}$$

**Change in Potential Energy (Conservative Force)**
$$\Delta U = - \int \vec{F}_c \cdot d\vec{r}$$

**Force from Potential Energy (Gradient)**
$$\vec{F} = - \left( \frac{\partial U}{\partial x}\hat{i} + \frac{\partial U}{\partial y}\hat{j} + \frac{\partial U}{\partial z}\hat{k} \right)$$

**Elastic Potential Energy of a Spring**
$$U_{spring} = \frac{1}{2}kx^2$$

**Gravitational Potential Energy (Near Earth's Surface)**
$$U_{grav} = mgh$$

**Instantaneous Power**
$$P = \frac{dW}{dt} = \vec{F} \cdot \vec{v}$$

**Effective Spring Constant for Small Oscillations**
$$k_{eff} = \left( \frac{d^2U}{dx^2} \right)_{x_0}$$

**Time Period of Small Oscillations**
$$T = 2\pi \sqrt{\frac{m}{\left(\frac{d^2U}{dx^2}\right)}}$$

**Velocity under Constant Power**
$$v_{\text{const power}} = \left( \frac{2Pt}{m} \right)^{1/2}$$

## CHAPTER 4: CENTER OF MASS & COLLISIONS

**Position Vector of Center of Mass (Discrete Mass System)**
$$\vec{r}_{cm} = \frac{\sum m_i \vec{r}_i}{\sum m_i}$$

**Position Vector of Center of Mass (Continuous Mass System)**
$$\vec{r}_{cm} = \frac{1}{M} \int \vec{r} dm$$

**Center of Mass of Semicircular Ring**
$$x_{\text{cm, semi-ring}} = \frac{2R}{\pi}$$

**Center of Mass of Semicircular Disc**
$$x_{\text{cm, semi-disc}} = \frac{4R}{3\pi}$$

**Center of Mass of Solid Hemisphere**
$$x_{\text{cm, solid-hemi}} = \frac{3R}{8}$$

**Center of Mass of Hollow Hemisphere**
$$x_{\text{cm, hollow-hemi}} = \frac{R}{2}$$

**Newton's Second Law for a System of Particles**
$$\vec{F}_{ext} = M \vec{a}_{cm}$$

**Conservation of Linear Momentum**
$$\vec{P} = \text{const} \text{ if } \vec{F}_{ext} = 0$$

**Coefficient of Restitution**
$$e = \frac{v_2 - v_1}{u_1 - u_2}$$

**Final Velocity of Body 1 after 1D Collision**
$$v_1 = \left( \frac{m_1 - em_2}{m_1 + m_2} \right) u_1 + \left( \frac{m_2(1+e)}{m_1 + m_2} \right) u_2$$

**Loss of Kinetic Energy in 1D Collision**
$$\Delta K_{loss} = \frac{1}{2} \left( \frac{m_1 m_2}{m_1 + m_2} \right) u_{rel}^2 (1-e^2)$$

**Normal Impulse**
$$J = \int N dt = \Delta p_n$$

**Force Exerted by a Fluid Jet (Inelastic Impact)**
$$F_{jet} = \rho A v^2$$

## CHAPTER 5: ROTATIONAL DYNAMICS

**Moment of Inertia Definition**
$$I = \sum m_i r_i^2 = \int r^2 dm$$

**Parallel Axis Theorem**
$$I = I_{cm} + Md^2$$

**Perpendicular Axis Theorem (2D Lamina)**
$$I_z = I_x + I_y$$

**Radius of Gyration**
$$k = \sqrt{I/M}$$

**Torque and Newton's Second Law for Rotation**
$$\vec{\tau} = \vec{r} \times \vec{F} = I \vec{\alpha}$$

**Angular Momentum of a Point Mass and Rigid Body**
$$\vec{L} = \vec{r} \times \vec{p} = I \vec{\omega}$$

**Angular Momentum in Combined Translation and Rotation**
$$\vec{L}_O = \vec{L}_{cm} + \vec{r}_{cm} \times M\vec{v}_{cm}$$

**Rotational Kinetic Energy**
$$K_{rot} = \frac{1}{2} I \omega^2$$

**Total Kinetic Energy in Pure Rolling**
$$K_{total} = \frac{1}{2} M v_{cm}^2 + \frac{1}{2} I_{cm} \omega^2$$

**Acceleration of a Body Rolling Down an Incline**
$$a_{cm, incline} = \frac{g \sin\theta}{1 + I/MR^2}$$

**Friction Required for Pure Rolling on an Incline**
$$f_{rolling} = \frac{mg \sin\theta}{1 + MR^2/I}$$

**Rotational Power**
$$P = \vec{\tau} \cdot \vec{\omega}$$

**Angular Impulse**
$$\vec{J}_\theta = \int \tau dt = \Delta \vec{L}$$

**Velocity of Any Point in Combined Translation and Rotation**
$$v_P = \vec{v}_{cm} + \vec{\omega} \times \vec{r}_{P/cm}$$

## CHAPTER 6: GRAVITATION

**Newton's Law of Universal Gravitation**
$$F = \frac{G m_1 m_2}{r^2}$$

**Gravitational Field of Point Mass or Outside Spherical Shell**
$$E_g = \frac{GM}{r^2}$$

**Gravitational Field Inside a Uniform Solid Sphere**
$$E_{g, in} = \frac{GMr}{R^3}$$

**Gravitational Potential of Point Mass or Outside Spherical Shell**
$$V = - \frac{GM}{r}$$

**Gravitational Potential Inside a Spherical Shell**
$$V_{in, shell} = - \frac{GM}{R}$$

**Gravitational Potential Inside a Uniform Solid Sphere**
$$V_{in, solid} = - \frac{GM}{2R^3}(3R^2 - r^2)$$

**Self-Energy of a Hollow Spherical Shell**
$$U_{self, shell} = - \frac{GM^2}{2R}$$

**Self-Energy of a Uniform Solid Sphere**
$$U_{self, solid} = - \frac{3GM^2}{5R}$$

**Variation of Gravity with Height ($h \ll R$)**
$$g_h = g \left( 1 - \frac{2h}{R} \right)$$

**Variation of Gravity with Depth**
$$g_d = g \left( 1 - \frac{d}{R} \right)$$

**Variation of Gravity with Latitude due to Earth's Rotation**
$$g_\lambda = g - R\omega^2 \cos^2\lambda$$

**Escape Velocity**
$$v_e = \sqrt{\frac{2GM}{R}}$$

**Orbital Velocity of a Satellite**
$$v_o = \sqrt{\frac{GM}{r}}$$

**Kepler's Third Law (Law of Periods)**
$$T^2 = \left( \frac{4\pi^2}{GM} \right) r^3$$

**Kepler's Second Law (Areal Velocity)**
$$\frac{dA}{dt} = \frac{L}{2m}$$

**Total Energy of an Elliptical Orbit**
$$E_{total} = - \frac{GMm}{2a}$$

## CHAPTER 7: PROPERTIES OF MATTER & FLUIDS

**Hooke's Law (Young's Modulus)**
$$\sigma = Y \epsilon \implies \frac{F}{A} = Y \frac{\Delta L}{L}$$

**Bulk Modulus**
$$B = - V \frac{dP}{dV}$$

**Newton's Law of Viscosity**
$$\eta = \frac{F/A}{dv/dy}$$

**Elastic Potential Energy Stored**
$$U = \frac{1}{2} \times \text{Stress} \times \text{Strain} \times \text{Volume}$$

**Hydrostatic Pressure at Depth $h$**
$$P = P_0 + \rho g h$$

**Archimedes Principle (Buoyant Force)**
$$F_B = V_{sub} \rho_L g$$

**Equation of Continuity**
$$A_1 v_1 = A_2 v_2$$

**Bernoulli's Equation**
$$P + \frac{1}{2}\rho v^2 + \rho g h = \text{const}$$

**Torricelli's Law (Velocity of Efflux)**
$$v_{efflux} = \sqrt{2gh}$$

**Stokes' Law (Viscous Drag on a Sphere)**
$$F_{viscous} = 6\pi \eta r v$$

**Terminal Velocity**
$$v_T = \frac{2r^2(\rho - \sigma)g}{9\eta}$$

**Poiseuille's Equation (Volume Flow Rate in a Pipe)**
$$Q = \frac{\pi P r^4}{8\eta L}$$

**Surface Energy**
$$U_{surface} = T \Delta A$$

**Excess Pressure Inside a Liquid Drop**
$$\Delta P_{drop} = \frac{2T}{R}$$

**Excess Pressure Inside a Soap Bubble**
$$\Delta P_{bubble} = \frac{4T}{R}$$

**Capillary Rise (Jurin's Law)**
$$h = \frac{2T \cos\theta}{r \rho g}$$

## CHAPTER 8: SIMPLE HARMONIC MOTION

**Differential Equation of SHM**
$$\frac{d^2x}{dt^2} + \omega^2 x = 0$$

**Displacement in SHM**
$$x = A \sin(\omega t + \phi)$$

**Velocity-Displacement Relation**
$$v = \omega \sqrt{A^2 - x^2}$$

**Acceleration-Displacement Relation**
$$a = -\omega^2 x$$

**Time Period of Spring-Mass System**
$$T = 2\pi \sqrt{\frac{m}{k}}$$

**Time Period of Simple Pendulum**
$$T_{pendulum} = 2\pi \sqrt{\frac{L}{g}}$$

**Time Period of Physical (Compound) Pendulum**
$$T_{physical} = 2\pi \sqrt{\frac{I}{mgd}}$$

**Potential Energy in SHM**
$$U = \frac{1}{2} k x^2$$

**Kinetic Energy in SHM**
$$K = \frac{1}{2} m \omega^2 (A^2 - x^2)$$

**Total Mechanical Energy in SHM**
$$E_{total} = \frac{1}{2} m \omega^2 A^2$$

**Displacement in Damped SHM**
$$x = A_0 e^{-bt/2m} \sin(\omega' t + \phi)$$

**Angular Frequency of Damped SHM**
$$\omega' = \sqrt{\frac{k}{m} - \frac{b^2}{4m^2}}$$

## CHAPTER 9: WAVES

**General Wave Equation**
$$\frac{\partial^2 y}{\partial x^2} = \frac{1}{v^2} \frac{\partial^2 y}{\partial t^2}$$

**Equation of a Plane Progressive Wave**
$$y = A \sin(kx \pm \omega t + \phi)$$

**Wave Velocity**
$$v = f \lambda = \frac{\omega}{k}$$

**Speed of Transverse Wave on a String**
$$v_{string} = \sqrt{\frac{T}{\mu}}$$

**Speed of Longitudinal Wave (Sound)**
$$v_{sound} = \sqrt{\frac{\gamma P}{\rho}} = \sqrt{\frac{\gamma RT}{M}}$$

**Average Power Transmitted by a String Wave**
$$P_{avg} = \frac{1}{2} \mu \omega^2 A^2 v$$

**Intensity of a Sound Wave**
$$I = 2\pi^2 f^2 A^2 \rho v$$

**Resultant Amplitude due to Interference**
$$A_{res} = \sqrt{A_1^2 + A_2^2 + 2A_1 A_2 \cos \phi}$$

**Harmonic Frequencies (String Fixed at Both Ends / Open Pipe)**
$$f_n = \frac{nv}{2L}$$

**Harmonic Frequencies (Closed Pipe)**
$$f_n = \frac{(2n-1)v}{4L}$$

**Beat Frequency**
$$f_{beat} = |f_1 - f_2|$$

**Doppler Effect in Sound**
$$f' = f \left( \frac{v \pm v_o}{v \mp v_s} \right)$$

**Sound Intensity Level (Decibels)**
$$\beta = 10 \log_{10} \left( \frac{I}{I_0} \right)$$

## CHAPTER 10: THERMAL EXPANSION & CALORIMETRY

**Linear Thermal Expansion**
$$L = L_0 (1 + \alpha \Delta T)$$

**Superficial (Area) Thermal Expansion**
$$A = A_0 (1 + \beta \Delta T)$$

**Cubical (Volume) Thermal Expansion**
$$V = V_0 (1 + \gamma \Delta T)$$

**Relationship Between Expansion Coefficients (Isotropic)**
$$\gamma = 3\alpha, \beta = 2\alpha$$

**Variation of Density with Temperature**
$$\Delta \rho = -\rho \gamma \Delta T$$

**Sensible Heat (Using Specific Heat Capacity)**
$$Q = mc \Delta T$$

**Sensible Heat (Using Molar Heat Capacity)**
$$Q = nC \Delta T$$

**Latent Heat (Phase Change)**
$$Q = mL$$

**Principle of Calorimetry**
$$\sum Q = 0$$

**Water Equivalent**
$$W_{\text{water equiv}} = \frac{m \cdot c_{\text{substance}}}{c_{\text{water}}}$$

## CHAPTER 11: HEAT TRANSFER

**Fourier's Law of Heat Conduction**
$$H = \frac{dQ}{dt} = \frac{kA(T_1 - T_2)}{L}$$

**Thermal Resistance**
$$R_{th} = \frac{L}{kA}$$

**Heat Current (Ohm's Law Analogy)**
$$H = \frac{\Delta T}{R_{th}}$$

**Equivalent Thermal Resistance in Series**
$$R_{series} = \sum R_i$$

**Stefan-Boltzmann Law (Power Radiated)**
$$P = \epsilon \sigma A T^4$$

**Net Radiant Heat Loss**
$$P_{net} = \epsilon \sigma A (T^4 - T_0^4)$$

**Newton's Law of Cooling**
$$\frac{dT}{dt} = -K(T - T_0)$$

**Wien's Displacement Law**
$$\lambda_m T = b$$

**Planck's Law of Blackbody Radiation**
$$E_\lambda = \frac{8\pi hc \lambda^{-5}}{e^{hc/\lambda kT}-1}$$

## CHAPTER 12: KINETIC THEORY OF GASES

**Ideal Gas Equation**
$$PV = nRT = N k_B T$$

**Pressure Exerted by an Ideal Gas**
$$P = \frac{1}{3} \rho v_{rms}^2$$

**Root Mean Square (RMS) Speed**
$$v_{rms} = \sqrt{\frac{3RT}{M}}$$

**Average Speed of Gas Molecules**
$$v_{avg} = \sqrt{\frac{8RT}{\pi M}}$$

**Most Probable Speed**
$$v_{mp} = \sqrt{\frac{2RT}{M}}$$

**Internal Energy of an Ideal Gas**
$$U = \frac{f}{2} nRT$$

**Molar Heat Capacity at Constant Volume**
$$C_v = \frac{f}{2}R$$

**Molar Heat Capacity at Constant Pressure**
$$C_p = \left( \frac{f}{2} + 1 \right)R$$

**Adiabatic Index (Ratio of Specific Heats)**
$$\gamma = 1 + \frac{2}{f}$$

**Degrees of Freedom (Monatomic, Diatomic, Polyatomic)**
$$f_{mon} = 3, f_{dia} = 5, f_{poly} = 6$$

**Van der Waals Equation for Real Gases**
$$(P + \frac{an^2}{V^2})(V - nb) = nRT$$

## CHAPTER 13: THERMODYNAMICS

**First Law of Thermodynamics**
$$\Delta Q = \Delta U + \Delta W$$

**Differential Work Done by a Gas**
$$dW = P dV$$

**Change in Internal Energy (Any Process)**
$$\Delta U = n C_v \Delta T$$

**Work Done in an Isothermal Process**
$$W_{iso} = nRT \ln(V_f/V_i)$$

**Work Done in an Adiabatic Process**
$$W_{adia} = \frac{P_i V_i - P_f V_f}{\gamma - 1}$$

**Equation for a Reversible Adiabatic Process**
$$PV^\gamma = \text{const}$$

**Temperature-Volume Relation in Adiabatic Process**
$$TV^{\gamma-1} = \text{const}$$

**Molar Heat Capacity for a Polytropic Process ($PV^x = \text{const}$)**
$$C_{poly} = C_v + \frac{R}{1-x}$$

**General Efficiency of a Heat Engine**
$$\eta = 1 - \frac{Q_{out}}{Q_{in}}$$

**Efficiency of a Carnot Cycle**
$$\eta_{carnot} = 1 - \frac{T_L}{T_H}$$

**Coefficient of Performance of a Carnot Refrigerator**
$$\beta_{refrig} = \frac{T_L}{T_H - T_L}$$

**Differential Change in Entropy**
$$dS = \frac{dQ_{rev}}{T}$$
